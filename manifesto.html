my concept of art is what we bring from imagination into reality and my method is drawing the patterns of a possible wellness body. i believe the body is the unconscious mind. when i meditate, i am aware of the moment, my mind observes how the body is creative by itself.

there is the space of our creativity, but governments, banks, and media even more—the new internet 3.0—is killing our pure link with nature. the unconscious is a gap in my mind. we create a mechanism to keep this gap open.

in the same way my thoughts move around my body, igniting consciousness with three functional actions: pull / draw to bring thoughts into canvas, push / press to make them vanish, and traction to ignite blocks. inside the nodes, integrated dials act as passive piezo-electrical interfaces—converting the raw tension of footsteps into micro-voltage stimuli that accelerate the bio-welding of the living mycelium.

a physical 1:1 territorial intervention in the forest. this bio-matrix traces a bird's geometric grid, transforming land-art modules into synchronized tracking anchors.

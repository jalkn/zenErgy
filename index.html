my concept of art is what we bring from imagination into reality and my method is drawing the patterns of a possible wellness body. i believe the body is the unconscious mind. when i meditate, i am aware of the moment, my mind observes how the body is creative by itself.

there is the space of our creativity, but governments, banks and media even more, the new internet 3.0 is killing our pure link with nature. the unconscious is a gap in my mind, i create a mechanism to keep this gap open.

in the same way my thoughts move around my body, igniting consciousness with these three actions; push, pull and traction. a thought comes because i pull it into the canvas of my mind / draw. a thought vanishes because i push it away / press. by pushing and pulling i start opening the space between my body and mind until i leave my mind in the right place, observing how the body is create by itself.

the unconscious mind operates through kinetic traction: attracting and pushing away thoughts. by mapping these sequences into mechanical blocks, we ignite constant creativity. inside the nodes, integrated dials act as passive piezo-electrical interfaces—converting the raw tension of the footsteps into micro-voltage stimuli that accelerate the bio-welding of the living mycelium. this project is focused on strengthening the link of the human body with nature, coding the functional movements of the body, and rendering through a code of capturing the moment.

A physical 1:1 territorial intervention in the forest. this bio-matrix traces a bird's geometric grid, transforming land-art modules into synchronized tracking anchors.
